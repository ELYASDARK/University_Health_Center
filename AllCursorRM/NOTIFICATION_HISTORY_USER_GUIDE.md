## ‚ùì FAQ

**Q: How long are notifications kept?**
A: Notifications are kept indefinitely until you delete them.

**Q: Can I recover deleted notifications?**
A: No, deletion is permanent. Be careful with "Delete all"!

**Q: Why don't I see any notifications?**
A: 
- Check notification permissions in settings
- Ensure the app has permission to send notifications
- You may not have any appointments or waitlist entries yet

**Q: The badge count seems wrong?**
A: Pull down to refresh the notification list. If still incorrect, try:
- Closing and reopening the app
- Checking your notification permissions

**Q: Can I turn off certain notification types?**
A: Currently, notification preferences are managed in your profile settings. Future updates will add more granular controls.

**Q: Do notifications work offline?**
A: 
- You can view notification history offline (if loaded previously)
- New notifications require an internet connection
- Actions (mark as read, delete) sync when you're back online
